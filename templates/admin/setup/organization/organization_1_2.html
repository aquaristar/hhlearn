The following information pertains to the organization as a whole. We will allow you to further define your organization in upcoming pages.

When it comes to grading tests on HHLEARN we allow you to decide what the passing scores are. We have 4 different types of tests: General Knowledge, Continuing Education, Employee Competency, and your own Custom Courses.

HHLEARN has a Monthly Safety Inservice Program that is available to all organizations. This provides a short (usually 30 minutes) course once a month on a safety topic. The topics are pertinent to their job descriptions and have 12 courses preselected for each job description. If you have the Professional version of HHLEARN, these 12 courses can be modified for each job description. In the Professional version your organization also has the ability to turn this feature on and/or off for every aspect of your organization, e.g., company, region, location, and department.
