Welcome to HHLEARN!

In order to start using your new learning management system, your organization must load information into the system in order for us to be able to customize your courses, data and experiences.

HHLEARN is extremely flexible when it comes to being setup, which means you can set it up with minimal detail or provide greater information to benefit from a more refined learning and managing experience. Don't be hesitant to set it up simply in the beginning because you can always adjust the setup settings and features any time.

Above, you will see a progress bar that will follow and update as you progress through the loading steps. Below and to the right of each progress bar there is a "Watch Me" icon that will provide you with a quick tour of the page you are currently completing. All steps must be completed (whether simply or in great detail) before organizations may start loading users into the system.

You do not have to complete he entire setup process at one time; HHLEARN will keep track of your setup progress and will direct you to where you left off—just make sure you save the page you are working on prior to logging out.

Again, we wish you a warm welcome. Shall we get started?
