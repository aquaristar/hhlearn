Pricing

A subscription to HHLEARN is only pennies a day. The price to subscribe is based on the range of employees that are loaded. The pricing points have been designed for common organizational sizes. There is no prorating of each employee on the system. Once an employee is deactivated they cannot be reactivated. Their records are stored, but cannot be utilized like an active employee. This limitation is only used to prevent organizations from turning employees on and off to keep their employee limits within the subscribed quantity.
