COURSE ASSIGNMENTS

In about one minute an employee can be loaded into the system and be automatically assigned a course curriculum that is designed for their specific job description.

COURSE CATEGORIES

HHLEARN's courses are methodically constructed, thoroughly tested, designed by industry professionals, and professionally written for the adult learner.

COURSE TYPES

The courses on HHLEARN fall into three different types: general knowledge, job-specific knowledge, and continuing education.

COURSE CUSTOMIZATION

HHLEARN has features designed so that each organization can modify an existing course curriculum (or start from scratch) to meet its exact needs.
