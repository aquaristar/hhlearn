  • AN·DRA·GŌ·GY

    Andragogy emphasizes the value of the process of learning. Malcolm Knowles (theorist of adult education), defined andragogy as "the art and science of helping adults learn."

  • Adult-Learning Principles

    HHLEARN uses the six principles of adult learning defined by Knowles: autonomous and self-directed, life experiences and knowledge, goal-oriented, relevancy-oriented, practical, and respected.

Features HHLEARN's building blocks are modules.  Modules are the key to training effectively.

We built the #MostAccurateLMS by incorporating a modular approach. HHLEARN has a myriad of modules that allow the system administrator to assign the appropriate modules to the organization, to a specific location, to a specific department, and even to an individual learner. By using a modular approach, your staff has access to the modules they need to improve their KSAs, and your organization only pays for the essential modules; saving money and providing better education, woo-hoo!

Simplicity

HHLEARN takes the guesswork out of employee education and training. HHLEARN is designed so all that organizations have to do is assign a job title. Once a job title is applied, a course curriculum is assigned. Employees can start training immediately.

Flexibility

HHLEARN can be tailored to match each organization's design. The HHLEARN system has the flexibility to uniquely setup individual locations, departments, regions, and even employees; you are never stuck with general settings that affect everyone.

Turn on and off

Modular design makes turning on features easy. Custom Monthly Safety Program*, Publications Library (System, DRM*, and Custom*), and Videos Library (System and Custom*). *Requires PRO Edition.

Law of the land

The home health industry (homecare, homehealth, DMEPOS, hospice, and pharmacy) is regulated to the point of being overwhelming. HHLEARN has simplified this! We track federal, state, county, and city laws, in addition, we track accreditation organization standards. We link all of these regulations to each system resource.
