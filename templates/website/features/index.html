WHAT ELSE CAN HHLEARN DO?

MODULAR DESIGN

HHLEARN breaks content into smaller chunks that can be combined in unique ways for each learner. These learning modules build upon each other and take an employee from general knowledge to specific job duties.

SELF-PACED

Employees may learn at a pace that is just right for them. Employees can speed up, slow down, or even challenge a course at any time.

ACCESSIBILITY

HHLEARN provides an Internet-based e-learning system available to subscribers 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year, from anywhere with Internet access.

MULTIPLE LEARNING STYLES

Home care, home medical equipment, hospice, and pharmacy personnel are as unique as we are diverse and they all have different ways of learning. HHLEARN accommodates this by making available multiple auditory and visual delivery methods geared to different learning styles.

ACTIVE NOT PASSIVE

HHLEARN is dynamic, which means the course is customized on the fly for the individual and has instant messaging capability during the course.

BIMODAL PRESENTATION

Bimodal presentation (audio and visual) improves reading comprehension, information recall, and learning/memory enhancement.
