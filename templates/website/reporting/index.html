All reports on HHLEARN are created in real-time and have many customizing attributes. At certain time intervals key employees receive emails to alert them to specific information that is ready to view, which includes what's being completed and what's not being performed. HHLEARN stores these reports so they are available for viewing at any time and for benchmarking data. HHLEARN also records each time a key employee opens or downloads these key reports. How empowering would you feel when you as a company owner can document whether a manager is viewing the reports that pertain to the employees they manage?

REPORT STORAGE

Almost every report that can be displayed on HHLEARN can be stored for later viewing and archiving. If you can print previous reports that recorded data at previous periods in time, an organization can increase its knowledge and use this knowledge to make future decisions.

DASHBOARD

HHLEARN's dashboard provides a quick overview that is tailored for each user. The dashboard has key features like storing personal and professional information, which includes tracking cumulated continuing education hours based on the professional's renewal period. The dashboard also keeps track of priority courses and their assigned dates and the user's most recent course completions.

STATISTICS

The statistics column tracks each individual's percent completion and weighted grade point average, which can be extrapolated into a letter grade, and compares it to their assigned location.

SMART REPORTING

HHLEARN automatically tailors each report to contain the data that is appropriate for the viewer. If a user is in charge of three locations, their reports will display only data that pertains to those three locations. If you are the organizational administrator, your reports will display data that pertains to the entire organization.

SYSTEM ALERTS

Employee messages can be sent to the entire organization, separate locations, different departments and regions, and all the way down to a specific employee. Each of these messages must be acknowledged and the system tracks the receipt and non-receipt of each message.

COMPLIANCE REPORTING

Compliance reporting mandates affect all homecare industries, such as HIPAA, accreditation, benchmarking, and employee education, and a well-designed compliance reporting solution can aid in meeting compliance reporting guidelines.
