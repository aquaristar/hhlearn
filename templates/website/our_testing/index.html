TRUE TESTING

A true test of an e-learning site's value is the retention of the information taught. Almost every e-learning site allows users to download the course and test and then come back at a later time and take the test. This is not learning! This is called a paper push. Allowing users access to courses and tests offline is tantamount to cheating and provides a piece of paper that is usless.

COMPETENCY

In the pool of questions for each course are questions coded as competency questions, which mean they never show up on general knowledge exams. When it is time to test an employee's overall competence, the competency questions from the various courses are pooled to generate a competency examination. This means users are accountable for every course they complete and no employee will receive the same competency test.

GENERAL KNOWLEDGE

General knowledge tests account for a majority of the exams on HHLEARN and test the overall knowledge of a course that is neither a continuing education or competency exam.

CONTINUING EDUCATION

Continuing education tests are examinations that have continuing education credits and provide professionals with FREE training to meet professional licensing requirements.

CERTIFICATES OF COMPLETION

HLEARN has developed different certificates of completion (COC) for each type of test. The system is designed so applicable certificates are provided to each user. A user can receive multiple COCs for the successful passing of one exam. An example would be the completion of a CE test; since each COC has different requirements, the user would receive a general knowledge and a continuing education certificate.

Each of the certificates of completion is emailed to each individual upon successful completion of the test. In addition to emailing the COCs, HHLEARN stores each certificate in portable document format (PDF) for printing at any time. The passing percentage for each type of test is adjustable for each organization. Test taking on HHLEARN is the most flexible and yet simple in the e-learning industry.
