Course Regulatory Documentation

Name:  John Smith
Course:   Introduction to HHlearn
Completed:   06-01-2012
Course Summary: This course is written for all individuals that are employed by healthcare organizations. This course is provided as part of a monthly safety education program that has the purpose of assisting employees in learning and understanding topics that will help prevent personal injuries, illnesses, and conditions. This course covers slip, trip, and fall (STF) prevention, which includes the top 10 hazards, explanations on how they contribute to STFs, the identification of where the top 10 hazards are likely to occur, and recommendations to reduce or eliminate STF hazards. This course is not intended to be an STF prevention program, however, it does provide guidance on creating and implementing one. By understanding the topics discussed in this course, you can better protect yourself and your fellow employees.
Course Objectives:
  • Be able to identify current work-related slip, trip, and fall statistics.
  • Be able to identify the top 10 slip, trip, and fall (STF) hazards.
  • Be able to identify where the top 10 STFs are likely to occur.
  • Be able to identify recommendations to reduce or eliminate STF hazards.
  • Be able to identify the tools and resources Best Medical Supply can utilize to implement an STF prevention program.
  • Be able to identify elements and information on implementing an STF prevention program.
  • Be able to identify resources that can help codify and track Best Medical Supply's STF incidents.
  • Be able to identify the methods of and purpose of STF employee training.
  • Be able to identify the definitions for the highlighted glossary words.
